Level Up Your Gaming Skills!

Want to improve your gaming performance? Check out these tips and tricks for some of my favorite games.

It Takes Two - Mastering Co-op Gameplay

  • Communication is key - always talk to your partner.
  • Experiment with different character abilities for creative solutions.
  • Timing and coordination make puzzles easier.
  • Adapt to changing mechanics as the game introduces new abilities each level.
  • Use the environment to your advantage for solving puzzles and uncovering hidden paths.
  • Take breaks if you're stuck—sometimes fresh eyes help you spot the solution.

Lethal Company - Survive the Horrors

  • Stick together - splitting up makes you an easy target.
  • Use sound cues to detect threats before they find you.
  • Plan escape routes before entering dangerous areas.
  • Use headphones for better audio cues that help you locate enemies.
  • Stay calm—panic makes it harder to think and react quickly.
  • Always know where your hiding spots are to avoid being caught off guard.

Minecraft - Build & Survive Like a Pro

  • Start with a shelter before nightfall to stay safe.
  • Mine deep for diamonds, but bring torches to light your path.
  • Always carry a water bucket to survive falls and lava.
  • Collect resources efficiently by mining in a zig-zag pattern to uncover more.
  • Craft a shield early to defend against mobs and other players.
  • Enchant your gear for improved protection and effectiveness in combat.

Stardew Valley - Farm, Fish, and Friendships

  • Maximize your farm layout by grouping crops together and leaving space for sprinklers.
  • Upgrade your tools early—prioritize the pickaxe and watering can.
  • Focus on building relationships by giving gifts, especially on birthdays.
  • Save money for winter when crops won't be growing, and use the time to mine and fish.
  • Use sprinklers to automate watering and make farming more efficient.
  • Take advantage of the Greenhouse to grow crops year-round for extra profit.

Among Us - Master the Art of Deception

  • Communicate effectively—share important information without chaos.
  • As a Crewmate, complete tasks quickly and stick with others to create an alibi.
  • Watch for suspicious behavior, like players avoiding tasks or standing still.
  • As an Impostor, sabotage strategically to create confusion and opportunities for eliminations.
  • Don't be overly defensive when accused; it can make you look guilty.
  • Keep track of the body count and the number of players alive to make informed voting decisions.
